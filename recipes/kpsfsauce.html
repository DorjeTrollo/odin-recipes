Killer Peanut Stirfry Sauce

This best peanut stirfy sauce you'll find anywhere. So good people have dropped dead from pure pleasure after tasting it.

Try it and see if I'm wrong.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 cup tamari
  • 1/4 crunchy peanut butter
  • 4-1/2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Combine water, tamari, peanut butter, brown sugar, lemon juice, garlic, and pepper flakes.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Pour over your veggies/meat/whatever your heart desires!
  4. Try your best not to die from the mouthgasm you are experiencing.
