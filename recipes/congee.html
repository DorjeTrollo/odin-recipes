This congee will knock you off your feet and make you feel like a 12 year old falling in love for the first time. It's tastier than your first kiss with Brandon, that's for sure.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons safflower oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced ginger
  • 1/4 lbs shitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice once and drain the water.
  2. Heat the oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Add the minced ginger and garlic and cook for 30 seconds, until they start to become fragrant. Add the mushrooms and rice, and saut√© for another minute.
  3. Carefully pour the broth and water into the pot. Sprinkle in the salt and stir. Bring the broth to a boil, uncovered. Then, turn the heat to low and cover the pot. Let the congee simmer for about an hour, stirring occasionaly.
  4. After an hour, check the congee to see if the consistency is to your liking. Taste the congee and add more salt if necessary.
  5. Serve the congee in bowls. have sliced scallions, fried shallots and chili oil on the side for topping.
